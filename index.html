About

Tadiwanashe George Mangondo is a Zimbabwean billionaire entrepreneur, renowned for his expertise in forex trading, cybersecurity, web design, and philanthropy. A self-made success, he built his fortune through innovative forex strategies and has since expanded into cybersecurity, helping protect businesses globally.

As a web designer, he has transformed digital experiences, creating user-friendly, cutting-edge websites for various industries. Beyond business, he remains committed to giving back, working tirelessly to shape a better future for Zimbabwe and beyond.

Early Life

Tadiwanashe George Mangondo was born on 30th August 1998 into a humble Zimbabwean family. From a young age, he exhibited a strong desire to make a meaningful impact, dreaming of achieving success on his own terms. Despite facing financial constraints and limited access to resources, he remained determined, believing that education and innovation were the keys to overcoming adversity.

By the age of 20, Tadiwanashe had already established himself as a rising star in the world of finance. His journey into forex trading began in his teenage years, where his curiosity and entrepreneurial spirit led him to master the intricacies of the financial markets. His early success in trading laid the foundation for his wealth-building strategies.

His passion for technology and security led him to pursue higher education, culminating in his graduation from the Harare Institute of Technology in 2028, where he earned first-class honors in Information Security and Assurance. His entrepreneurial mindset, cultivated during his high school years, continued to drive him throughout his academic journey, allowing him to merge his interests in finance and technology to pave the way for future successes.
